Upcoming Events

Poker Run Event

May 6, 2024

Here at Livsey's Rec ATV and OHV, we are hosting our annual Poker Run! Riders will race to various checkpoints collecting playing card along their journey. At the end of the ride, riders will assemble their hands and the best poker hand will win a grand prize of $1,000. Second place will win a prize of $250 and third place will recieve a $50 Amazon gift card. Must be 18+ years of age to enter the competition. To register, please call us or visit our contact us page and fill out the forum or click here, and will get back to you as soon as possible.

Glowing Night Ride

June 15, 2024

The night adventure of the year. Bring your glowsticks, lanterns, LED whips, flashlights, light bars, and sound systems for the spookiest and most adventurous night of the summer.
